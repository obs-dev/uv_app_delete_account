Account Deletion – UVapp

This page applies to the UVapp mobile application.

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from the app:

  • Open UVapp
  • Go to 'Me' Page → Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion

Data deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account is deleted
  • Your Firestore user data is removed
  • No data is retained
